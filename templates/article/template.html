Abstract
A version of Carleman's formula for functions holomorphic in a rectangle is proved. It is applied to the evaluation of the integral of $\zeta$-function logarithm with the summing factor $\exp(-t)$ along the critical line. This allowed to obtain a new statement equivalent to the riemann hypothesis.
Keywords
$\zeta$-function; Carleman's Formula
